About Me

My name is Meyli Colmenero (메일리), and I am a Senior studying Computer Engineering. I enjoy making and learning whether it's Web Design, Robotics, or General 3D Modeling. I want to know it all!

I am currently interested in roles that merge hardware and software; however, my experience, schooling, and optimism can guide me to other roles when the work sounds really interesting as well.
